How Can We Help You?

How to Order

  1. Go to Place Order from the sidebar
  2. Browse available cookies and select quantities
  3. Review your order in the summary panel
  4. Choose your payment method (Cash or GCash)
  5. Click Place Order to submit

Payment Methods

Cash Payment: Pay at the counter when you pick up your order.

GCash Payment: Pay via GCash mobile wallet. You'll receive payment instructions after placing your order.

Order Status

Track your order status in real-time:

  • Pending: Order received, waiting for preparation
  • Preparing: Your cookies are being prepared
  • Ready: Your order is ready for pickup
  • Completed: Order has been picked up

Loyalty Points

Earn points with every purchase! Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

Check your points balance in the Loyalty Points section.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Yes, you can cancel your order before it starts being prepared.

Q: How long does it take to prepare my order?
A: Most orders are ready within 15-30 minutes depending on the size.

Q: Do you offer delivery?
A: Currently, we only offer pickup. Delivery options may be available in the future.

Q: What if a cookie is out of stock?
A: Out-of-stock items will be clearly marked. You can only order available items.
